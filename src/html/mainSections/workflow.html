Workflow for your project

  • 1. Requirements Gathering: Discuss with the client or customer's team the goals and functional requirements of the website. Determine key features, design, functions, target audience, and other important aspects.

  • 2. Market and Competitor Research: Conduct an analysis of competitive websites and market trends to understand how to make your website unique and appealing to the target audience.

  • 3. Design Creation: We develop the website design, including page layouts, color palette, and interface elements. We provide you with the designed concept for your approval, and only after your endorsement, we proceed to the development phase. If you have any suggestions for adjustments or refinements to the design, we are always ready to collaborate and incorporate necessary changes according to your vision and preferences. Your involvement in this process is a significant stride toward crafting the perfect web project.

  • 4. Development and Programming: At this stage, we initiate the website development using the chosen technologies. Pages, functionality, and, if needed, databases are created. We conduct testing on various devices and browsers to ensure its smooth operation.

  • 5. Launch: Once the website has successfully passed testing, we proceed to host it and configure the domain. We ensure that all links, images, and functionalities are working as intended. We will conduct basic optimization for search engines (SEO) to make it more discoverable to users through search engines.

  • 6. Support and Maintenance: After the website is launched, we maintain its functionality, update content, and are capable of adding new features and improvements based on the client's needs.
