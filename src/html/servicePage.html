WHICH WEB SITE TO CHOOSE?

  • Landing Page

    This type of resource is created with the aim of focusing the visitor's attention on the core information being provided and motivating them to take action (such as making a call, submitting a form, placing an order, etc.). It's ideally suited for sales and service offerings. It differs from other websites by omitting complex navigational elements, distracting menus, and extensive text blocks.

  • Portfolio Website

    The Best Solution for Those Who Want to Showcase Themselves and Their Abilities. The project itself consists of 10 - 20 web pages that present information about the company, collaboration terms, and much more that can characterize the project. This tool will enable the target audience worldwide to become acquainted with your unique business proposition.

  • Catalog

    There are no significant differences visible to the visitor between an online store and a catalog, apart from the pricing. The main distinction lies in the presence of optimized business processes within the store and their absence in the catalog. If a customer requires consultation when purchasing your product, creating a catalog would be the better solution. An online store, on the other hand, shifts the order formation process onto the buyer. .

  • Corporate website

    This type of website is most suitable for companies and various types of organizations. Features of corporate websites can include sections with employee portfolios, profiles of executives, and photographs of top managers of the month. Blocks containing the company's slogans and its developmental milestones are also commonly present on corporate portals.

  • Online store

    At the present moment, developing an online store website is the optimal solution for any business based on sales. The future of global commerce lies in this direction. It offers several advantages compared to a traditional brick-and-mortar store:
    • Convenience of Access: Customers can visit from any corner of the world.
    • Time Savings: Shopping can be done without leaving the comfort of one's home, thus saving time.
    • Easy Product Search: Navigational menus make finding products convenient.
    • Higher Profit Margins: Increased profits from sales and reduced costs in terms of establishment and maintenance.

  • Custom Project

    If none of the aforementioned options suit your needs, you have the opportunity to order a website with individual characteristics, functionality, etc. Web studio specialists will gladly bring all your ideas to life, regardless of their complexity!
